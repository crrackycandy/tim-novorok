Centrifugal Electric Pump

This is an electric pump what will be used to supply fuel to a 600lbf rocket engine. This pump is what I am building in a group of 4 people for our senior project. The project was started in the Spring 2026 semester, and is expected to be completed by the end of 2026. It is currently in the early design phase. The pump needs to provide a head rise of 800 psi, a flowrate of 9 gpm, and a runtime of 12 seconds. We are planning to utilize a Barske type impeller and a LMT electric motor for the pump.
