ASI - Nitrous/Methane Augmented Spark Igniter

This is an ASI that runs off methane and nitrous oxide gas. Its design includes a spark plug, check valves, solenoids, and other parts as needed. Propellant flow calculations were done in excel, and the solenoids are controlled via an arduino. A custom adapter had to be built to load methane into a tank.
