Engine 2 - 600lbf Nitrous/IPA Liquid Rocket Engine

This is the second rocket engine made by Hornet Aerospace & Propulsion. It is designed to produce 600 lbf, at 600 psi. This project was started in the Spring 2025 semester, and is currently being assembled/tested.
